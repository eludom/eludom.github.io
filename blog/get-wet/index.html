1500 miles down, 700 to go

2020-05-25 / outdoors

Of course, I have some of the hardest miles left: the Smokies, Mt. Washington, the Whites, the Presidentials, the Bigelows, but with persistence, luck, health (and some HARD hiking) I should finish in a few years.
